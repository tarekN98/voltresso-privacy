Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

The short version: Voltresso stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information with third parties. Your brew logs, recipes, and preferences never leave your phone.

1. Information We Collect

Voltresso collects the following information that you provide directly:

  • Name and email address — used to create your in-app profile. Stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Brew logs — espresso shot parameters you record. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Saved recipes and custom recipes — stored locally on your device only.
  • Machine selection and preferences — stored locally on your device only.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on our servers:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your brew history or logs
  • Your recipe library
  • Your location
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data

3. AI Chat (Volt AI)

The Volt AI feature sends your chat messages to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Messages sent to Volt AI are processed by Anthropic in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We do not store your chat history on our servers. Chat history exists only in your device's session memory and is cleared when you close the app.

Your selected espresso machine context is included in AI requests to provide relevant advice. This is limited to the machine name and its technical parameters.

4. Push Notifications

Voltresso may request permission to send you push notifications with brew reminders and barista tips. You can grant or revoke this permission at any time in your device's Settings. We do not use push notifications for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

All personal data (name, email, brew logs, recipes, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using React Native AsyncStorage. This data is subject to your device's built-in security (encryption, passcode protection). We do not have access to this data and cannot recover it if your device is reset.

6. Children's Privacy

Voltresso is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a revised date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all Voltresso data at any time by uninstalling the app. This permanently removes all locally stored data.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
